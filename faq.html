FAQs – SecuraNet AI
  • What is SecuraNet AI?
    SecuraNet AI is an intelligent cybersecurity platform designed specifically for African public and educational institutions. It leverages artificial intelligence to detect and prevent threats such as phishing, file tampering, email spoofing, and clone websites. The system aims to make cybersecurity accessible, automated, and effective even in low-resource environments.
  • What services does SecuraNet AI provide?
    SecuraNet AI offers four key cybersecurity services:

    File Management & Monitoring: to detect suspicious file activity.
    Clone Site Detection: to protect users from fake websites.
    Phishing Detection: to prevent scams and fraudulent messages.
    Email Header Checker: to analyze hidden email metadata for authenticity.

    These tools work together to protect users, systems, and data from both common and complex cyber threats.
  • How does the Email Header Checker work?
    The Email Header Checker analyzes the technical metadata of an email — such as sender address, IP paths, SPF/DKIM status, and message routing — to determine if the message may be spoofed or fraudulent. It flags potential risks, traces sender locations, and helps users verify if an email should be trusted before clicking on any links.
  • What is Clone Site Detection?
    Clone Site Detection identifies fraudulent websites that closely mimic legitimate platforms like university portals or government sites. Using AI and pattern recognition, it scans links, content structure, and domains to detect fake versions designed to steal credentials or distribute malware. This protects users from being tricked by lookalike pages.
  • How does File Management enhance cybersecurity?
    The file management system continuously monitors file interactions within a platform — such as uploads, downloads, renames, and deletions — to detect and report abnormal or risky behavior. It can flag infected files, prevent unauthorized changes, and log activity for auditing, helping institutions maintain data integrity and prevent internal threats.
  • Who is SecuraNet AI built for?
    SecuraNet AI is developed for students, teachers, administrators, and IT teams in African universities, public schools, and government departments. It's especially useful for organizations with limited cybersecurity capacity, offering tools that are easy to use, multilingual, and adaptable to different levels of digital infrastructure.
  • Why is SecuraNet AI important?
    Africa's growing digital landscape is increasingly vulnerable to cyberattacks. SecuraNet AI addresses this by offering a practical, locally-aware solution that helps institutions prevent common cyber threats. By combining AI with offline access and multilingual support, it reinforces the continent's cyber resilience and protects digital education and public service systems.
